Pintura: Geographical Digital Graffiti App

June 2017




About

Pintura is a web app in which users can post and view digital graffiti based on their current location. This project was a part of COGS 121: HCI Programming Studio, where we worked in teams of 4 to create geographical and map-related applications. The other members of my team include Connie Guan, Tony Salim, and Chang Gwoun.
