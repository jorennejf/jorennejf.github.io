Sophomoreland Brochure

June 2017

The students decorated some lockers around campus with the brochures I made. This is the final printed-out brochure opened up to display the map.

Overview


Client

John F. Kennedy High School Class of 2020

Goal

The goal of this project was to create a themed experience for the students.


    Tools

  • Adobe Photoshop

Story

The Sophomoreland Brochures were maps directly related to the ones given out at Disneyland in Anaheim, CA. I kept the same names and colors and only moved the locations of the attractions and areas to fit in with the campus buildings and planned homecoming decorations (e.g. Splash Mountain).

I made this project to help my younger sister with homecoming decorations at John F. Kennedy High School; their class theme was Disneyland. I created the design entirely on my own using the already created logo, but my sister gave me the details of where to put certain attractions and areas.

The cover includes the logo, and the first flap contains information on the other homecoming themes for the week. The entire map of campus was conceptualized into "Sophomoreland" with Disneyland's different areas and attractions.
