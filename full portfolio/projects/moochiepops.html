Moochie Pops and Cakes Marketing Materials

Apr 2019


Overview


Client

Moochie Pops and Cakes


Goal

The goal of this project was to design a business card and flyer to help a local business promote their bakery.


    My Roles

  • Vector Graphics
  • Visual Design

    Tools

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator

Story

A colleague had approached me to help her create a business card to hand out to current and potential customers. She already had a logo for me to include in the design, which she wanted to look "professional, like other bakeries, but not too whimsical".

Later, she asked me to also create a flyer with a similar design to show off some of the desserts she makes.


Design Process


  1. Using the original logo provided, I created this design with all the basic info and a classy pattern.
    Version 1 - Front
    Version 1 - Back

  2. After seeing the first version, the client asked if I could add other desserts with "cute faces" on them, so that new customers would see the variety of desserts they provide. She also asked if I could remake the logo so that she can have a higher quality one to use.
    Version 2 - Front
    Version 2 - Back

  3. In this final version, I removed the faces on the desserts and instead gave them a few more toppings because the client and I agreed that the faces drew us away from our original goal of making the design look professional. Additionally, she wanted to add some text to clarify what it is their business does.
    Version 3 - Front
    Version 3 - Back

  4. Since the client was very happy with the business card design I made, she also asked me to create a flyer of the same design. She provided me with some photos of their desserts to showcase on the flyer.
    Flyer

Conclusion

I definitely feel like the challenge in vector graphics is figuring out what exactly it is that you want to make. I don't know much about cake decorating, so I had to look up a bunch of dessert pictures to decide how to design these (virtual) desserts. It took a couple tries, but I'm glad the client was happy with the design and wanted to continue working with me even beyond this project.
