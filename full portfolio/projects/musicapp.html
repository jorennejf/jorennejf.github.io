Music App Interactive Prototype

Nov 2017

This is a screenshot of our interactive prototype on InVision.

Overview


Goal

The goal of this project was to create a user-friendly music app based on research of other popular music apps.


    My Roles

  • User Research
  • Competitive Analysis
  • Information Architecture
  • User Interface Design
  • Interactive Prototyping

    Tools

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • InVision
  • Google Docs

Story

Our project is an interactive prototype based off of user research of frequent music listeners and competitive analysis of other music apps. The features we designed were limited to creating and managing a playlist.

This project was a part of COGS 187A: Usability and Information Architecture, in which we worked in a team of 3. The other members of my team include Connie Guan and Ricky Lieu.


Design Process


    Interview

  1. As a team, we came up with interview questions to ask our users. After asking these questions, we observed our interviewees interacting with some of our competing apps: Spotify, Amazon Music, or Soundcloud. We interviewed 9 people total and used these interviews to come up with user scenarios.

    2. Comparisons

  2. To gain a better understanding of what does and doesn't work, we tried out our user scenarios with other music apps. We used annotations (pictured below) to highlight key components of the app that were necessary to complete the scenarios.

I really enjoyed creating the UI of all the pages of our app, but it was a challenge connecting them all together on InVision because there always seems to be a page missing in between. In the end, we got a 96% on this assignment. Feedback from the TA said that we only lost those points because we didn't follow the directions exactly and forgot one small thing.
