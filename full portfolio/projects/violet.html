Story

Violet is a mobile app that offers peer-to-peer valet services. Drivers can get their car parked at any location and valets can earn money parking cars. Originally, under a different name, the company was a platform for electric vehicle owners to share charging stations, but our user research showed that this would not have been a viable service. We switched to valet services and found more positive feedback. In the end, our team won first place during the final class pitch competition.

This project was a part of COGS 122: Interaction Design Startup, where we worked in groups to create our own mobile service startup. The other members of my team include Michael Tolentino, Kevin Jan, and Marissa Hing.


Branding

With this brand, we tried to go for a more professional look. This way, we can get one of our stakeholder classes (the drivers) to trust the company.


Landing Page

Using our branding, we made a landing page on Unbounce. We used our landing page to analyze interest in our service.

Conclusion

Making your own startup from scratch is a lot of work. I don't know too much about business, but I was lucky to work with a team that had more knowledge about this. I think our team had a diverse set of skills that helped our company win the first place prize (chocolate, popcorn, and fake money).
